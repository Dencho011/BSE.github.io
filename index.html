Use Fries in a Sentence

“Grammar Fries”

Procedure:

1. At the bottom of each fries, there was a word written and they will construct their own sentence using those words. Most of the words are written using the basic eight (8) Parts of Speech.

2. The class will be divided into three (3) groups.

3. The members of each group will pick a fries and pull it out of the fries box.

4. They will be using the words they picked up to construct a sentence.

5. At first the student will be given a chance to pick up to five (5) pieces of fries. If the pieces will not be enough to form a sentence the students is given a chance to replace the words they don’t like by bringing the word back to the box and getting a new one.

6. After some attempts, the students will successfully create and construct their own sentences.

Rules:

1. The points will be based on the number of words the student used in constructing his/her sentences. There are a maximum of 5 pieces fries so the maximum points on each attempts will be 5 points.

2. The students can also use a lesser number of words in constructing his/her sentence (e.g. Natalya is beautiful.). It is not necessary to use all of the 5 pieces of fries as long as you can form a sentence then that’s counted.

3. There will be a timer of 2 mins. Only for each student.

4. The group who will get the highest points will be the winner.

5. This activity is for 30 mins. only and can be used for a quiz or an activity.

Hanging Bag of information

Description:

  • This IM is known as a Hanging Bag of Information, and it contains the lesson or topic that the teacher will discuss in class. However, at the Sadness section, I have printed letters that I will give to my students for their records. I'll also hand out the printed worksheets at the end of class for evaluation.

“You watch to Learn”

Description:

  • By the use of television where it capture the students attention, they will learn how to read silent letters and know why it is pronounce it that way and learn when such words or letters must be read in silence. The teacher can use this IM by rotating the steel in the side part of the television.

“Mystery Box”

Description:

  • This IM is known as Mystery Box there is two mystery boxes the other box contains of different topics and the other one is for the theme. The students need to pick from the box that contains the topic and the other one is the theme that they need to use for the topic that they picked. Example: The topic that they pick is about online class and the theme that they pick is about courage and perseverance they need to write an essay for it.

“Social Communication Game”

Description:

  • The students will pick a number from 1-10, each number has an equivalent challenge like question, introducing, describing, trivia and say it with different tone. This game is about their selves so that it practices their self in speaking and they will gradually develop their confidence.

I Heard You”

Description:

  • The students will listen to the story then the teacher will ask the students about the lesson of the story, tone, mood and the purpose of the author.

“POP UP BOOK”

Description:

  • Viewing is one of the learning competencies where it is a thing designed to be seen, viewed or watched. The pop up book tells about the “Dead Stars” by Paz Benitez. The short story entitled “Dead Stars” is an example of literature from the Philippines. I also included the different elements of short story.
